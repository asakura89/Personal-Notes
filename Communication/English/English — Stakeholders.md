## Secara umum

Stakeholder ini adalah orang-orang yang terlibat dan berkepentingan di suatu project, suatu organisasi, atau suatu inisiatif tertentu. Stakeholder ini bisa terpengaruh secara direct atau indirect dari hasil-hasil dan keputusan-keputusan terkait dengan project tersebut. Contoh karyawan, customer, supplier, investor, regulator, anggota masyarakat, dan lainnya. Stakeholder sering memiliki tingkat pengaruh atau influencial yang berbeda. Dan sudut pandang serta kebutuhan mereka harus diperhitungkan buat memastikan project-nya berhasil.



## Dalam IT projects

In the context of an IT project, stakeholders encompass various individuals and groups who are associated with or impacted by the project's design, development, implementation, and operation. Here are some specific examples of stakeholders in an IT project:

### 1. Business Users

These are individuals or teams within the organization who will use the IT solution to achieve their business goals and tasks. They provide insights into the functional requirements and usability of the system.



### 2. Project Sponsor

The person or group that provides the financial resources, support, and overall direction for the project. The project sponsor is usually a senior executive or leader within the organization.



### 3. Project Manager

Responsible for planning, executing, and closing the project. The project manager ensures that the project meets its goals, stays within scope, and adheres to timelines.



### 4. Development Team

IT professionals who design, develop, test, and implement the IT solution. This may include software developers, programmers, and system analysts.



### 5. Testing Team

Individuals who perform quality assurance and testing to ensure that the IT solution functions correctly and  meets the specified requirements.



### 6. End Users

The people who will directly use the IT solution once it's implemented. Their feedback and needs are crucial for designing user-friendly interfaces and functionalities.



### 7. IT Support Team

Responsible for maintaining, troubleshooting, and providing technical support for the IT solution post-implementation.



### 8. Regulatory Authorities

If the IT project involves compliance with specific regulations or industry standards, regulatory authorities become stakeholders with a vested interest in the project's adherence to those requirements.



### 9. External Vendors/Partners

Third-party suppliers or vendors who provide software, hardware, or services for the project.



### 10. Customers/Clients

If the IT solution is customer-facing, customers or clients using the solution become stakeholders, influencing its design and functionality.

Understanding and managing the needs, expectations, and contributions of these stakeholders is crucial for the success of an IT project. Effective communication, collaboration, and engagement with stakeholders help ensure that the project aligns with business objectives and delivers value to all parties involved.
