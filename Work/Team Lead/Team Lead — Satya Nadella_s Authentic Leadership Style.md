# Satya Nadella's Authentic Leadership Style

## Leadership Journey

Karir Satya Nadella di Microsoft dimulai di tahun 1992, sebagai engineer. Bertahun-tahun, dia bertumbuh jadi leader yang produktif dan visioner. Leadership style-nya dianggap sukses. Semuanya berakar dari kemampuannya untuk memahami existing culture lalu terus memperkenalkan new values, norma-norma, dan shared assumptions buat ngebikin culture baru.

Tiga role penting yang dia lakuin adalah:
1. <ins>Mengakui perlunya revitalisasi</ins>
2. <ins>Menciptakan visi baru</ins>
3. <ins>Melembagakan perubahan (institutionalizing change)</ins>



## Impact on Microsoft

Kepemimpinan Nadella punya dampak signifikan ke Microsoft dari dua hal. Performance dan innovation. Pendekatan "Never Say NO"-nya dan supportive style-nya ber-impact ke employee empowerment. Yang ujungnya jadi nge-increase revenue dan total assets-nya Microsoft.

Semenjak Nadella jadi CEO di 2014, dia mengubah company's culture dengan menekankan beberapa hal ini:
1. <ins>Pemberdayaan individu (individual empowerment):</ins> Leadership Nadella nge-promote culture dimana karyawan didorong buat mengekspresikan ide-ide mereka dan bikin keputusan. Menumbuhkan kreativitas didalam organisasi. Dia juga menanamkan culture inovasi, dengan cara mendorong karyawan biar secara kritis memeriksa market dan nge-develop ide-ide inovatif. Akhirnya, Microsoft beralih dari fase status quo ke accelerated rate of growth. Di bawah Nadella, Microsoft jadi perusahaan paling berharga keempat di dunia.
2. Positivity
3. Empati
4. Employee growth
5. Trust and equitable growth
6. <ins>Diversity and Inclusivity:</ins> Komitmennya terhadap keragaman dan inklusivitas dibuktiin dengan dukungannya untuk kampanye "Black Lives Matter". Dia dapat pengakuan sebagai salah satu "Best CEOs for Diversity 2020".
7. Sustainability



## Authentic Leadership

Authentic leadership karakternya ada 4 komponen:

1. <ins>Self-awareness</ins>
2. <ins>Relational transparency</ins>
3. <ins>Internalized moral perspective</ins>
4. <ins>Balanced processing</ins>

Kaya yang udah dijelasin di atas, Nadella ini leader yang empatik dan inklusif. Yang menghargai keragaman dan mendorong generasi ide-ide inovatif. Juga dia punya skill interpersonal yang kuat dan highly rated  oleh rekan-rekannya di Microsoft.



### Self-Awareness

Self-awareness ini key skill buat para authentic leaders. Leaders harus ngerti strengths dan weakness dari seseorang, tau dan aware gimana orang lain memandang mereka, dan ngerti impact mereka terhadap orang lain. Leaders dengan high self-awareness bisa meng-influence atau mempengaruhi perilaku dan pikiran member mereka, memotivasi mereka, dan nge-build relationship dan trust, yang ujungnya mengarah ke hubungan interpersonal yang positif. Yang juga pada akhirnya, promotes creativity.

This openness provides an opportunity for followers to express their opinions, ideas, and challenges¹[1]²[2]. It also creates an environment of honesty, openness, and trust, which promotes creativity among followers and encourages them to generate new ideas.

He openly communicates his expectations to his employees in three simple approaches: Create clarity, Strike energy, and Ability to achieve success³[3]. **Relational Transparency and Internalized Moral Perspective in Leadership**

Relational transparency and an internalized moral perspective are two more important skills for authentic leaders. Relational transparency involves expressing consistent moral values and standards, even when faced with social, organizational, and team pressures. Leaders with this skill align their behaviors and decisions with their internalized values. They promote integrity and honesty, discourage unethical behaviors, and foster a positive climate and trust, which in turn encourages creativity¹[1].

Satya Nadella, the CEO of Microsoft, exemplifies these leadership skills. ### Balanced Processing in Leadership

Balanced processing is a crucial skill for authentic leaders. It involves objectively analyzing all available information before making a decision. This approach improves fairness in decision-making, promotes positive interpersonal relationships, and builds trust. Ultimately, it fosters an emotional attachment among followers towards their organizations and enhances creativity¹[1].

Satya Nadella, the CEO of Microsoft, exemplifies balanced processing in his leadership style. He is known for his collaborations with rival firms, taking risks to encourage teamwork and growth²[2]. This approach has fostered trust in his leadership role and enhanced creativity within the company³[3]. One of his initiatives, aimed at bringing education back on track by working with a partner, has been highly appreciated by his followers⁴[4].

Nadella's three meeting rules - listen more, talk less, and be decisive - have become very popular⁵[5]. These rules reflect his balanced approach to processing information and making decisions. He aims to unify the various aspects of Microsoft into a cohesive whole.

After examining the changing work environment, Nadella stated that the world is transitioning from traditional work culture to a new hybrid work system⁶[6]. He calls this the new regular work, which focuses on results rather than the number of logged hours⁷[7]. This decision has encouraged employees to be more productive and has fostered a sense of attachment to Nadella, particularly during the pandemic⁸[8].


**Practical Contributions of Nadella's Leadership Style**

The analysis of Nadella's leadership style offers several practical contributions. First, leaders should allow followers the freedom to think and behave independently. Second, leaders should foster trust and build positive interpersonal relationships to promote creativity. Third, leaders should provide followers with the freedom and opportunity to express their opinions and ideas²[2]. Fourth, leaders should promote integrity and honesty, discourage unethical behaviors, and foster a positive climate and trust³[3]. Fifth, leaders should foster an emotional attachment among followers towards their organization⁴[4]. Sixth, leaders should focus on collaboration, learning, empowerment, positivity, empathy, and growth of employees⁵[5]. Lastly, leaders should ensure that followers' voices are heard and a good communication channel is integrated to develop a culture of trust that can promote creativity⁶[6]. These suggestions can help leaders enhance their leadership skills and organizational performance⁷[7]. They are applicable not only to IT giants but also to any other business in the 21st century⁸[8].
