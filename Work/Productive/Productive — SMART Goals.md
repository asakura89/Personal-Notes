# SMART Goals


I have a project that has 60 open tickets. can you help define smart goals examples to effectively increase ticket closing-rate. also please ceate a way to monitor and measure the progress. you can create a template with a list or a table.

Sure, I can help you with that. SMART goals are goals that are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant and Time-bound. They help you to set clear and realistic objectives that can be tracked and evaluated. Here are some examples of SMART goals for increasing ticket closing-rate¹²³⁴⁵:

- Specific: I will close 10 tickets per day by following the ticket resolution process and escalating issues when necessary.
- Measurable: I will use the ticketing system to monitor my daily progress and report my weekly performance to my manager.
- Achievable: I have the skills, knowledge and resources to close 10 tickets per day, which is within the average range of my team.
- Relevant: Closing more tickets will improve customer satisfaction, reduce backlog and support the team's overall goals.
- Time-bound: I will achieve this goal by the end of this month and review it for the next month.

To monitor and measure your progress, you can create a template like this:

| Date | Tickets closed | Issues escalated | Feedback received |
|------|----------------|------------------|-------------------|
| 1/1/2023 | 8 | 2 | Positive |
| 1/2/2023 | 9 | 1 | Positive |
| 1/3/2023 | 11 | 0 | Positive |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

I hope this helps you with your project.😊
