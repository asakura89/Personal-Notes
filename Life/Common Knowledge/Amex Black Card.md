What is the difference between Amex Black Card and other credit cards?


The **Amex Black Card** is an exclusive charge card offered to high-net-worth individuals by invitation only. It has a hefty $10,000 initiation fee and a $5,000 annual fee¹. The card offers many travel perks such as Global Lounge Collection access to more than 1,400 VIP lounges in more than 140 countries¹.

On the other hand, other credit cards such as the **Amex Platinum Card** are one tier lower and prospective cardholders may apply online and must meet minimum eligibility requirements. The Platinum Card has a $695 annual fee with many travel-related perks¹.

The main difference between the Amex Black Card and other credit cards is its exclusivity and the high-end benefits associated with it. Is there anything else you would like to know?
