# Arale

## Differences

The differences between Naruto run and Arale run are:
- Naruto run is a running style based on the way the characters run leaning forward with their arms outstretched behind them in the anime series Naruto[^1][^2]. It is also called the ninja run because it is supposed to make the runner faster and more aerodynamic[^1].
- Arale run is a running style based on the way Arale Norimaki, the protagonist of the manga and anime series Dr. Slump, runs with her arms flailing in the air and her legs kicking high[^3][^4]. It is also called the Arale Kick because she often uses it to launch a powerful kick on her opponents[^4][^5].

Some people may mix up both runs because they are both inspired by anime characters and involve unusual arm movements, but they are actually very different in terms of posture, speed, and purpose.

## Purposes

- The purpose of Naruto run is to **<u>[consume less energy](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=consume%20less%20energy)</u>** while running, and to **<u>[keep the body straight](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=keep%20the%20body%20straight)</u>** on the path as one is running from one point to the other[^1]. It is also supposed to make the runner **<u>[faster and more aerodynamic](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=faster%20and%20more%20aerodynamic)</u>**[^2], although some experts have disputed this claim[^3]. It is seen as the way **<u>[martial artists](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=martial%20artists)</u>** or **<u>[ninjas](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=ninjas)</u>** run, especially when they have a sword or a scabbard in their hand[^1].
- The purpose of Arale run is to **<u>[show her enthusiasm](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=show%20her%20enthusiasm)</u>** and **<u>[joy](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=joy)</u>** while running, and to **<u>[launch a powerful kick](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=launch%20a%20powerful%20kick)</u>** on her opponents when she is still in the air[^4][^5]. It is also a way of **<u>[expressing her personality](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=expressing%20her%20personality)</u>**, which is **<u>[na%C3%AFve, energetic, and playful](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=na%C3%AFve%2C%20energetic%2C%20and%20playful)</u>**[^5]. It is seen as the way **<u>[comical](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=comical)</u>** or **<u>[cute](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=cute)</u>** characters run, especially when they are in a **<u>[gag manga](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=gag%20manga)</u>** or **<u>[anime](https://www.bing.com/search?form=SKPBOT&q=anime)</u>** series[^4].

As you can see, Naruto run and Arale run have different purposes that reflect the different genres, tones, and themes of their respective series. Naruto run is more serious and action-oriented, while Arale run is more humorous and lighthearted. I hope this helps you understand the differences better.blush
