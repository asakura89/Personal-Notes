The differences between Naruto run and Arale run are:
- Naruto run is a running style based on the way the characters run leaning forward with their arms outstretched behind them in the anime series Naruto[^1][^2]. It is also called the ninja run because it is supposed to make the runner faster and more aerodynamic[^1].
- Arale run is a running style based on the way Arale Norimaki, the protagonist of the manga and anime series Dr. Slump, runs with her arms flailing in the air and her legs kicking high[^3][^4]. It is also called the Arale Kick because she often uses it to launch a powerful kick on her opponents[^4][^5].



Some people may mix up both runs because they are both inspired by anime characters and involve unusual arm movements, but they are actually very different in terms of posture, speed, and purpose.
