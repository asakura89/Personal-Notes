# Feeling down

Ini permasalahan semua orang. Gak ada orang yang gak pernah feeling down. Apakah karena sedih, kecewa, terluka. Apapun kisahnya, suka bikin uring-uringan dan gak ada semangat. Cara biar bersemangat lagi ya adalah kembali ke kenyataan dan penerimaan. Ketika udah nerima maka harusnya gak akan segampang itu buat merasa jatuh lagi.

1. Buat bisa menerima sepenuh hati, kita bisa coba debug akar masalahnya. Terus coba bikin daftar pro-kontranya



## Daily driver

Daily driver ini adalah hal, apapun, yang bisa nge-rive atau ngangkat motivasi kita tiap hari. Orang yang punya hobi, biasanya bakal bia nemuin hal-hal yang bisa bikin dia termotivasi lagi. Karena mereka akan bisa nyari pengalihan dari hal yang bikin mereka ngerasa down. Tapi ada juga yang masih bingung buat nyari sesuatu buat bisa dijadiin hobi. Atau malah sebenernya walaupun udah punya hobi, hobinya tetep gak bisa bantuin buat bikin reda perasaan down-nya.

Hal utama yang bisa dilakuin adalah cari hal-hal yang bisa bikin rileks. Mungkin hal-hal ini bisa ngebantu.
1. Bershowerlah, kalo kata @poconggg di twitter. Galau, bershowerlah.
2. Katanya, katanya nih, kata dokter deket rumah, kalo kita nyiram kepala pake air dingin, itu bisa bikin rileks. Efeknya sama kaya kalo lagi sakit kepala terus rambut yang daerah sakit kepalanya ditarik.
3. Seduh Teh. Bukan anak teh? Ngopi. Bukan anak senja yang doyan ngopi? Bikin susu hangat. Enggak juga? Coklat anget deh. Udah gausah banyak komplen.
4. Dengerin playlist akustik, piano yang slow bpm, atau playlist apapun yang bikin kita rileks.

Maka carilah daily driver, biar kita bisa termotivasi lagi di tiap harinya.



## How to be happy again?

Feeling down ini kan perasaan emosi yang berpengaruh ke mental kita. Kalo ngomongin mental, katanya, katanya nih, yang bisa nyembuhin penyakit mental itu diri kita sendiri. Psikolog atau orang terdekat kita itu cuma bisa dengerin, bantu nyari tau masalahnya dimana. Udah, gitu doang. Ibarat kata, mereka cuma bisa bantu debug, yang bisa bug-fix cuma diri kita sendiri.

Jadi gimana cara kita bisa happy lagi? semangatin lah diri sendiri. Ketika satu dunia ini turn their back on us atau againts us at all, yang bisa bantu dan nyelamatin diri kita adalah diri kita sendiri.

# Overcoming Feeling Down

## The Power of Acceptance

Accepting our situation may seem difficult, but it's a crucial step in moving forward. By identifying the root cause of our problem and weighing its pros and cons, we can gain a better understanding of our situation and begin to accept it. This process may involve talking to a therapist or trusted friend, journaling our thoughts and feelings, or practicing mindfulness.

Once we accept our situation, it becomes much harder for us to fall back into feeling down. Acceptance allows us to let go of negative emotions and focus on the present moment. It also empowers us to take positive action towards improving our situation.

For example, if we're feeling down because we're unhappy with our job, acceptance may involve acknowledging that our current job isn't fulfilling and exploring other career options. If we're feeling down because of a relationship issue, acceptance may involve communicating openly with our partner and working towards resolving the issue.

## Finding Your Daily Driver

A daily driver is anything that can drive or boost our motivation every day. For some people, this may be a hobby that provides a distraction from what's making them feel down. For others, it may be something as simple as taking a shower or listening to relaxing music.

The key is to find what works for you. Experiment with different activities until you find something that helps you relax and regain your motivation. It could be brewing some tea or coffee, taking a walk in nature, practicing yoga or meditation, or engaging in creative activities such as painting or writing.

It's also important to prioritize self-care and make time for activities that nourish your mind and body. This could include getting enough sleep, eating a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and engaging in relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or progressive muscle relaxation.

## How to Be Happy Again

Overcoming feeling down and finding happiness again is a journey that requires time and effort. Here are some steps you can take to help you along the way:

1. Practice gratitude: Take time each day to reflect on the things you're grateful for. This could include writing in a gratitude journal or simply taking a moment to appreciate the good things in your life.

2. Connect with others: Spend time with friends and loved ones who support and uplift you. Engage in activities that bring you joy and foster connection.

3. Set achievable goals: Set realistic goals for yourself and work towards achieving them. This could include personal or professional goals that give you a sense of purpose and direction.

4. Take care of your physical health: Make sure to eat well, exercise regularly, and get enough sleep. Taking care of your physical health can have a positive impact on your mental health.

5. Seek support: Don't be afraid to seek support from a therapist or counselor if you need it. They can provide guidance and support as you work towards overcoming feeling down and finding happiness again.

Remember: happiness is a journey, not a destination. By taking small steps each day towards acceptance, self-care, and connection with others, you can overcome feeling down and find joy in life once again.
