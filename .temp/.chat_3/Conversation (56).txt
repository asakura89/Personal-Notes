## Bing
There are different types of police canes, depending on the country and the purpose. Some common names for police canes are:
- Baton: A short, thick stick used as a weapon by law enforcement officers. It is also called a truncheon, nightstick, or club.
- Rattan: A thin, flexible cane made from the stems of a palm plant. It is used for corporal punishment in some countries, such as Singapore and Malaysia.
- Sharia cane: A long, thin cane used for public flogging in some Islamic countries, such as Indonesia and Saudi Arabia. It is applied to the back of the offender, who is usually clothed.
