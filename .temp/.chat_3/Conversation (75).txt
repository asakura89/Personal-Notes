What is alkaline electrolyzed water?
## Bing
Alkaline electrolyzed water is produced by the electrolysis of ordinary tap water containing dissolved sodium chloride. The electrolysis occurs in a special water ionizer machine that separates the water into two streams: alkaline and acidic. The alkaline stream has a higher pH than regular tap water, typically between 8 and 9.5, and contains minerals such as calcium, magnesium, and potassium. Some people believe that drinking alkaline water can help neutralize acid in the body and improve health, although there is limited scientific evidence to support these claims.
