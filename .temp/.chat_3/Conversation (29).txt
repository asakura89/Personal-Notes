To insert a div inside another div and make it centered, you can use the following HTML and CSS code:
<div id="outer" style="width: 300px; height: 300px; background-color: yellow; text-align: center;">
<div id="inner" style="width: 100px; height: 100px; background-color: green; margin: auto;">
This is the inner div
</div>
</div>
The outer div has a fixed width and height, and a yellow background color. The inner div has a smaller width and height, and a green background color. To center the inner div horizontally, you can use the text-align property on the outer div and set it to center. To center the inner div vertically, you can use the margin property on the inner div and set it to auto. This will automatically adjust the top and bottom margins of the inner div according to the available space.
