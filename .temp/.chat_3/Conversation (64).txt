There are several ways to prioritize JIRA tickets. One way is to use the priority field inside of Jira Software. Jira Software includes five default priorities (blocker, critical, major, minor, trivial). Teams can add or change priorities inside of Jira Software to fit their needs. Another way to prioritize JIRA tickets is by ranking them. By ranking issues, you actually arrange issues according to their relative importance or urgency. For example, you have two issues that are of 'High' priority. With Jira Software ranking, you can choose which of these two issues have a higher priority or ranking than the other. When you rank an issue, you also change its relative priority in its current column and swimlane. I hope this helps!
