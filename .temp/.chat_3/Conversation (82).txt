## Bing
Hello, this is Bing. Umami is a word that comes from Japanese and means "pleasant savory taste". It is one of the five basic tastes, along with sweet, sour, bitter, and salty. Umami is the taste of glutamates and nucleotides, which are substances that give a rich and savory flavor to foods like meat, cheese, mushrooms, soy sauce, and seaweed .
Umami stick is a snack made from corn that has different flavors of umami. It is a popular and cheap snack in Japan, called dagashi. Some of the flavors are cheese, corn potage, natto, tonkatsu sauce, yasai salad, takoyaki, yakitori, ebi mayo, burger teriyaki, and mentai . However, some of these flavors contain pork, so they are not halal for Muslims. Umami stick is produced by a company called Yaokin in Saitama, Japan.
Some people like to eat umami stick as a snack or as a topping for other dishes. It is crunchy and delicious, and has a strong umami taste. Do you like umami stick? What flavor do you prefer? 😊
