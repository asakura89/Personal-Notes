To freeze both rows and columns in Excel, you can follow these steps:
- Select the cell below the rows and to the right of the columns you want to keep visible when you scroll.
- Click on the View tab.
- Select the Freeze Panes command and choose "Freeze Panes."
This will lock the selected rows and columns in place, allowing you to scroll through the rest of the worksheet while keeping those rows and columns visible.
