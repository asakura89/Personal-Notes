Yes, I do. Madoka Magica is an anime series about a group of middle school girls who become magical girls. They are offered a contract by a cat-like creature to fight witches in exchange for a wish. However, they soon discover that being a magical girl is not as glamorous as it seems, and that they have to face the consequences of their choices. The series explores the themes of hope, despair, and friendship.
The anime has 12 episodes and was aired in 2011. It also has two recap movies and one sequel movie called Rebellion. There are also various manga spin-offs and video games based on the series.
