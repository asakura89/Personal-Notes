Here are the steps for making a delicious cup of pour-over coffee using a V60 or Kalitta brew method with a 16:1 ratio:
 - Use 25 grams of coffee and 400 grams of water.
 - Grind the coffee to a medium-fine consistency, similar to fine table salt.
 - Heat the water to between 195°F and 205°F.
 - Place a paper filter in the brewing device and rinse it with hot water.
 - Add the ground coffee to the filter and gently shake to level the grounds.
 - Pour enough water over the grounds to wet them evenly and let them bloom for about 30 seconds.
 - Slowly pour the remaining water over the grounds in a circular motion, avoiding pouring directly on the filter.
 - The total brew time should be between 3-4 minutes.
