## Bing
Miko (巫女) is a Japanese term that refers to a shrine maiden or a young priestess who works at a Shinto shrine. Miko were once likely seen as shamans but are now understood in modern Japanese culture to be an institutionalized role in daily life, trained to perform tasks ranging from sacred cleansing to performing the sacred Kagura dance.
